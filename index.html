Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our estates and services

Do you offer payment plans?
Yes, we offer flexible payment plans ranging from 3 to 12 months depending on the estate. Our plans are designed to make property ownership accessible without financial strain.
Are your properties government approved?
Absolutely. All our estates have proper documentation, including Certificate of Occupancy (C of O) or registered survey, depending on the location. We ensure full compliance with all government regulations.
How do I schedule an inspection?
You can schedule a physical or virtual inspection by calling our office at +234 812 345 6789 or by filling out the contact form on our website. Our team will coordinate a convenient time for you.
What locations do you operate in?
We currently have premium estates in Delta State (including Asaba, Ogwashi-Uku, and surrounding areas) and select locations in Lagos. We're constantly expanding to new premium locations.
